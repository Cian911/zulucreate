In-house Graphic Designer

We are a leading supplier of designer brand fragrances in Ireland including Skincare, Tanning, Health & beauty, Grooming and much more!

Graphic Designer with experience in Illustrator, Photoshop and Indesign is required for an in-house position in Kilkenny. You will be primarily responsible for the updating of our 248 page monthly sales catalogue and additional design elements involving all advertising material for the company, including beauty magazines, leaflets, booklets, brochures etc.

The ideal candidate will be a mid-level Graphic Designer and essentially with the following requirements
  • Degree/Diploma in Graphic Design
  • 2-4 years' experience working as a Graphic Designer or similar.
  • Proficiency with InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • Experience working on material such as brochures, leaflets, flyers, etc.
  • Experience working to tight deadlines and under pressure is required.
  • Must have excellent communication skills and be fluent in English.
  • Have an organised approach to work & be capable of multitasking.
  • Be able to priorities and work with minimal supervision.
  • Product photography experience an advantage.

For this roll the candidate must have a can do attitude, good communication skills and a problem solving approach. They must be passionate about design and take direction well from the M.D. They should have the confidence to give design options for any brief they receive.

Web design skills and experience is an advantage but not essential for this role as the majority of your work will be print based.
